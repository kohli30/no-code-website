Our Story

Founded in 2010, La Petite Boulangerie began as a passion project by two friends who shared a love for traditional French baking techniques and artisan craftsmanship.

Every morning at 4 AM, our skilled bakers arrive to prepare the day's offerings using time-honored methods passed down through generations. We source only the finest organic flours, locally-sourced butter, and seasonal ingredients to ensure each bite is nothing short of extraordinary.

Our commitment to quality means we never take shortcuts. From our 24-hour sourdough fermentation to our hand-laminated croissants, every product reflects our dedication to the craft of baking.

Today, we're proud to serve our community with the same passion and attention to detail that inspired us from the very beginning. Thank you for being part of our journey.
